About Me

Nobody is free from the world of visual art. It entertains us, comforts us, and manipulates us into buying garbage. My interest lies in visual entertainment such as comics and animations. I graduated from UCF with a BA in Studio Art, and recently learned how valuable online art courses can be. I like using many mediums so I have options when trying to create an image and Blender is the newest tool I am adding to my belt. I have some more features planned for this website the future but would like to organize my portfolio first and foremost. Feel free ask me anything about my work.
